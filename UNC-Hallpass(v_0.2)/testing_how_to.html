Testing Procedure

You can view on-campus Carolina Together Testing Centers and plan accordingly to keep your status compliant with the COVID-19 Community Standards.

Students taking classes on campus will be required to be tested regularly. This system will determine the frequency of those tests based on several factors.

You need to choose a location and schedule a testing hour. You can be tested anytime during that hour.

After arriving at a collection site, you will receive a test kit with a barcode. You will use your smartphone to scan the barcode and complete the test. At each testing center, you will be required to self-administer a COVID-19 nasal swab test while being monitored by the collection team. See the testing steps below.

TESTING PROCEDURE
1
REGISTER

To begin the process, you will be asked to submit the following information :

  • Preferred email address
  • Phone number
  • Local Address
  • Permanent Address
  • Quarantine Address
    •  In the event you are asked to quarantine, this address is where you will be located.
2
MAKE A RESERVATION

Next, choose the site at which you will report for testing.

Choose an available timeslot for your appointment and confirm. You may arrive at any point during your assigned time slot.

You will receive an email and text confirming your verification.

3
ARRIVE AT LOCATION

Report to your testing site with your QR code ready and you will be given a test kit.

HOW TO TEST

Report to your testing site with your QR code ready and you will be given a test kit. Follow the steps below in order to ensure an accurate test result.

1
BLOW NOSE (IF NEEDED) AND CLEAN HANDS
  • In order to get a good specimen, it's important that both your nose and hands are clean.
  • Using a clean tissue, gently blow your nose and clear out any additional debris in both nostrils.
  • Apply enough hand sanitizer to cover all surfaces of hands. Rub hands including palms, back of hands, and between fingers until all surfaces are dry.
2
OPEN NASAL SWAB
  • Remove the nasal swab from the wrapper. Many open like a band-aid, where you pull the ends of the wrapper apart.
  • Be careful to only touch the swab handle, not the tip.
3
SWAB NOSE
  • Gently insert the entire soft tip of the swab into one nostril until you feel a bit of resistance.
  • Using medium pressure, rub the swab slowly in a circular motion around the inside wall of your nostril five times.
  • The swab tip should be touching the inside wall of your nostril through each rotation.
  • Repeat the same process with the same swab in the other nostril.
4
PUT SWAB IN TUBE
  • While holding the swab, open the tube.
  • Put the swab tip first into the tube.
  • Break the swab at the break point. Leave the tip with the swab in the tube and throw away the plastic tip.
  • Screw the cap back onto the tube tightly.
  • Wash your hands again with hand sanitizer.
