Request for Release of COVID Test Results and Related Information :

The purpose of this document is to authorize The University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill (“UNC”) to disclose the specified information noted herein (including, if applicable, Protected Health Information, and all together “Information”) to the entities designated below, and I signify my agreement to this authorization (“Authorization”) by clicking in the box below.

I hereby authorize UNC and any of its agents, employees or representatives, to use and/or release personal information about me, including COVID-19 laboratory test results, contact information, demographic information, my class schedule, my close contacts (identified via contact tracing protocol), and application usage data. Such information may be used or disclosed for the purpose of treatment, payment, public health, operational, environmental health, reporting purposes, workplace medical surveillance, as required by applicable law, or any other purposes related to pandemic management for students, employees, or for UNC activities or programs.

Such information may be used by or released to the entities noted herein for the above purposes, including but not limited to, departments of public health, government agencies, health care providers, insurance companies, and various units of UNC (such as, for example, Campus Health Services, the Carolina Student Service Core, Employee Occupational Health Services, Institutional Integrity and Risk Management, Campus Housing, IT services, and others). UNC may release such information in any format requested.

Please read each of the following statements carefully before signing this document:

1. With respect to HIPAA PHI, I understand that I can revoke this Authorization at any time. I understand that revocation of this Authorization will apply and be effective only for future uses and disclosures of my HIPAA PHI related to COVID-19 laboratory test results, and any such revocation will not be effective for PHI that UNC has already used or disclosed in accordance with this Authorization. I understand that this Authorization shall be effective for 180 days from the signature date, unless revoked in writing by sending UNC a written notification to campushealth@unc.edu with the Subject Line “Revocation of HIPAA Authorization.”

2. I understand that information disclosed pursuant to this Authorization may be subject to re-disclosure by the recipient of such information. It is possible that, once disclosed, the privacy of the information may no longer be protected under federal or state medical privacy laws.

3. In addition, I consent to use of any reasonable form of communication regarding such information, including phone calls, emails, and text messages to the phone number and email I enter in this website, or any phone number or email UNC has associated with me. I further agree that this Authorization may be executed in electronic format and checking “Agree and Continue” below serves as my electronic signature. I further agree that UNC may log my agreement electronically. I understand I may print or screenshot this Authorization if my device so allows, and a copy is available here: hallpass.unc.edu where I may save or print a copy.

4. I understand that UNC will not condition medical treatment, any payment, enrollment in a health plan, or eligibility for benefits, on receiving my signature on this Authorization. Authorization hereunder is required for participation in the Carolina Together Testing Program.

5. I understand that this Authorization may be executed through the use of an electronic signature in accordance with the Electronic Signatures in Global and National Commerce Act (E-Sign Act), Title 15, United States Code, Sections 7001 et seq., the Uniform Electronic Transaction Act (UETA), and any applicable state law, and that any electronic signature shall be deemed an original signature for purposes of this Authorization, with such electronic signature having the same legal effect as an original signature.
